Our Laboratory Services

HAEMATOLOGY

Study and analysis of blood and blood-forming tissues. Complete blood counts (CBC), blood film examination, and other tests to diagnose anemia, infections, blood disorders, and monitor treatment progress.

  • Full Blood Count
  • Glucose-6-Phosphate Dehydrogenase
  • ESR
  • Total WBC
  • Hb / PCV
  • WBC Differentials
  • Malaria Parasite
  • Platelets Count
  • Blood Group & Rhesus
  • Genotype
  • Microfilaria
  • CD Count
Clotting Profiles
  • Bleeding Time
  • Clotting Time
  • Prothrombin Time
  • APTTK

MICROBIOLOGY

Culture, antimicrobial sensitivity testing, and pathogen identification to support infectious disease diagnosis.

  • Urine M/C/S
  • HVS M/C/S
  • Endocervical M/C/S
  • Urethral Swab M/C/S
  • Semen Analysis M/C/S
  • Sputum M/C/S
  • ZN Stain
  • Wound, Eye, Ear & Throat Swab M/C/S
  • Stool M/C/S
  • Urinalysis
  • Blood Culture
  • Pregnancy Test
  • Mantoux Test

CHEMICAL PATHOLOGY

Biochemical and metabolic investigations of blood and body fluids to assess organ function, monitor disease progression, and support accurate clinical diagnosis.

  • Liver Function Test
  • Renal Function Test
  • Diabetes Tests
  • Lipid Profile
  • Cardiac Function Test
  • Thyroid Function Test
  • Fertility Profile
  • Pancreatic Function Test

IMMUNOLOGICAL ASSAYS

Laboratory tests to detect and measure immune responses like antibodies and antigens. Helps in diagnosing autoimmune diseases, allergies, infections, and immune system disorders.

  • Tumour Markers
  • PSA Quantitative
  • AFP Qualitative
  • β-HCG
  • Helicobacter pylori

SEROLOGY

Detection of antibodies and antigens in blood samples. Commonly used for infectious diseases like hepatitis, HIV, typhoid, and others.

  • HIV 1 & 2
  • VDRL
  • Widal Test
  • Rheumatoid Factor
  • HBsAg

OTHER SPECIAL TESTS

Advanced and specialized investigations requested by clinicians to support complex diagnosis and research-based analysis.

  • AMH
  • Cortisol
  • Troponin I
  • D-Dimer
  • Vitamin-D
